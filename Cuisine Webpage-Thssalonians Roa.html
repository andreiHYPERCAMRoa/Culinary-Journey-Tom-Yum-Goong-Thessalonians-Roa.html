Tom Yung Goong:A Spicy Thai Delight

Tom Yung Goong is a classic Thai soup renowned for its bold flavor and aromatic spices.This hot and sour soup features a tantalizing blend of lemongrass,galangal, and kaffir lime leaves combined with succulent shrimp

"The key to a delicious Tom Yum Goong lies in the balance of the flavors-sweet,sour,salty,and spicy." Chef Somchai

Ingredients

Lemongrass: 2 Stalks

Thai Basil: 1 Cup

Kaffir Lime Leaves: 3 leaves

Shrimp: 500g

Preparation

1.Simmer the broth for ten minutes.

2.Add lemongrass, galangal, kaffir lime leaves.

3.Stir in the shrimp and cook until pink..

4.Season with fish sauces, lime juice, and chili paste.

5.Garnish with Thai basil and serve hot.

Cooking Tips

  • 1/4 cup of fish sauce to add intensity
  • 1 tablespoon of chili paste gives the red soup its signature heat
